Tom's Favorite movies ever!

These are the top five favorites!

Good Will Hunting

I absolutely love the story and Robin William's acting.

Forrest Gump

Love the history that is shown in the movie, plus seeing all the things Forrest does in his life.

Star Wars

Classic tale of good vs evil, plus amazing cinematography especially in the original trilogy. The films really were brought to life with a lack of CGI available at the time.

Harry Potter

Amazing story, love the fantasy world of magic that the film takes you into. I am a big fan of Allan Rickman as an actor also.

John Wick

Awesome action, and a very tragic main character
